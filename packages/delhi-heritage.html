Delhi Heritage Tour FAQs

Delhi has distinct seasons with different advantages:

  • October-March: Pleasant weather (8Â°C to 25Â°C), ideal for outdoor sightseeing
  • April-June: Hot days (up to 45Â°C) but good for early morning visits
  • July-September: Monsoon season - lush greenery but humid

Special periods: Republic Day (Jan 26), Independence Day (Aug 15), and Diwali offer unique cultural experiences.

Comfortable yet respectful clothing is recommended:

  • General: Light cotton clothes in summer, layers in winter
  • Religious Sites: Modest clothing covering shoulders and knees
  • Footwear: Comfortable walking shoes (easily removable for some sites)
  • Accessories: Hats, sunglasses and sunscreen for sun protection

Some mosques may require head covering for women (scarves provided at entrance).

Our comprehensive tour covers Delhi's most significant heritage sites:

  • UNESCO Sites: Qutub Minar complex, Humayun's Tomb, Red Fort
  • Mughal Era: Jama Masjid, Purana Qila, Safdarjung's Tomb
  • Colonial Delhi: India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan, Parliament House
  • Cultural Hubs: Chandni Chowk, Dilli Haat, Hauz Khas Village
  • Modern Marvels: Lotus Temple, Akshardham Temple (optional)

We customize itineraries based on your interests and available time.

We use multiple efficient transportation options:

  • Private AC Vehicle: For most transfers between major sites
  • Cycle Rickshaw: Authentic experience in Chandni Chowk lanes
  • Delhi Metro: Experience Asia's second-largest metro system
  • Walking Tours: For exploring compact heritage zones

All transportation is included in your package. We strategically plan routes to minimize travel time between sites.

Delhi offers incredible culinary diversity:

  • Street Food: Parathas in Chandni Chowk, Chaat at Bengali Market
  • Mughlai Cuisine: Karim's near Jama Masjid for authentic kebabs
  • Fine Dining: Heritage restaurants like Indian Accent
  • Vegetarian: Numerous options including popular chains
  • International: All major cuisines available

We can recommend and arrange meals based on your dietary preferences. Breakfast is included daily.
