Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

Arrive in Delhi, meet our representative and transfer to hotel. After some rest, proceed for full day Delhi sightseeing covering:

  • Red Fort (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Jama Masjid - India's largest mosque
  • Raj Ghat - Mahatma Gandhi's memorial
  • Qutub Minar - 73m tall victory tower
  • India Gate - war memorial
  • Lotus Temple - BahÃ¡'Ã­ House of Worship

Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Early morning drive to Agra (240 km / 4-5 hrs). Enroute visit Sikandra (Akbar's Tomb). After hotel check-in:

  • Visit Taj Mahal at sunset (closed Fridays)
  • Explore Agra Fort (UNESCO site)
  • Visit Mehtab Bagh for perfect Taj views

Optional: Sound & Light Show at Agra Fort. Overnight stay in Agra.

Sunrise visit to Taj Mahal. After breakfast, drive to Jaipur (240 km / 5-6 hrs) via:

  • Fatehpur Sikri - abandoned Mughal city (UNESCO site)
  • Abhaneri Stepwell - ancient water reservoir

On arrival in Jaipur, check in to hotel. Evening free at leisure. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Full day Jaipur sightseeing covering:

  • Amber Fort with elephant ride (optional)
  • City Palace - royal residence
  • Jantar Mantar - astronomical observatory
  • Hawa Mahal - Palace of Winds
  • Jal Mahal - water palace

Evening visit to local markets for shopping. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Morning free for last minute shopping. After lunch, drive back to Delhi (280 km / 5-6 hrs).

Drop at airport/hotel for onward journey.
