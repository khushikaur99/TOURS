Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

Early morning departure from Delhi (approx 6 AM). Arrive in Kalka by afternoon. Board the scenic toy train to Shimla (5-hour journey). Check into hotel upon arrival. Evening at leisure to explore Mall Road. Overnight in Shimla.

Full day exploring Shimla's attractions:

  • Visit the historic Viceregal Lodge
  • Explore Christ Church on the Ridge
  • Walk along Jakhoo Hill to the Hanuman Temple
  • Kufri excursion (seasonal snow activities)
  • Evening stroll on Mall Road and Scandal Point

Overnight in Shimla.

Morning drive to Manali (8-9 hours) through picturesque valleys. Stop at Pandoh Dam and Sunder Nagar Lake. Arrive in Manali by evening. Check into hotel. Free time to explore Mall Road. Overnight in Manali.

Full day exploring Manali's attractions:

  • Visit Hadimba Temple and Manu Temple
  • Explore Old Manali and Vashisht hot springs
  • Excursion to Solang Valley (optional adventure activities)
  • Visit Tibetan Monastery and handicraft center
  • Evening at leisure on Mall Road

Overnight in Manali.

Morning free for last minute shopping. After lunch, begin return journey to Delhi (overnight journey by Volvo bus or afternoon flight option available at extra cost).

Shimla Manali Tour FAQs

Shimla and Manali offer different experiences throughout the year:

  • March-June: Pleasant weather (10Â°C to 25Â°C), ideal for sightseeing
  • July-August: Monsoon season - lush greenery but possible roadblocks
  • September-November: Clear skies, best for photography and outdoor activities
  • December-February: Winter wonderland with snowfall (especially in Manali)

Peak seasons are May-June (summer vacations) and December-January (snow season).

Packing essentials vary by season:

  • Summer (Apr-Jun): Light woolens for evenings, comfortable walking shoes
  • Monsoon (Jul-Aug): Waterproof jacket, umbrella, quick-dry clothes
  • Autumn (Sep-Nov): Light jackets, layers for changing temperatures
  • Winter (Dec-Feb): Heavy woolens, thermals, gloves, snow boots

Regardless of season, bring sunglasses, sunscreen, and comfortable walking shoes.

The Shimla to Manali road trip takes about 8-9 hours:

  • Distance: Approximately 250 km
  • Route: NH205 via Bilaspur and Mandi
  • Road Condition: Mostly good with some winding mountain sections
  • Scenic Stops: Pandoh Dam, Sunder Nagar Lake, Hanogi Mata Temple

We provide comfortable AC vehicles with experienced drivers. Motion sickness tablets recommended for those prone to car sickness.

Solang Valley offers various seasonal adventure activities:

  • Summer Activities: Paragliding, zorbing, ATV rides, ropeway
  • Winter Activities: Skiing, snowboarding, snow tubing
  • Year-round: Horse riding, cable car rides

Note: Adventure activities are not included in package cost and are subject to weather conditions. Proper safety gear is provided by operators.

Popular shopping items and locations:

  • Shimla: Mall Road for woolens, handicrafts, jams; Lakkar Bazaar for wooden items
  • Manali: Mall Road for Tibetan handicrafts, shawls; Old Manali for hippie-style clothes
  • Specialties: Kullu shawls, Himachali caps, dried fruits, local honey

Bargaining is expected in most shops except branded outlets. Carry cash as some smaller shops don't accept cards.
