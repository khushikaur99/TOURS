Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

Arrive in Shimla, meet our representative and transfer to hotel. After some rest, proceed for local sightseeing covering:

  • The Ridge - the heart of Shimla
  • Mall Road for shopping and leisure
  • Christ Church - second oldest church in North India
  • Scandal Point - famous meeting point

Overnight stay in Shimla.

After breakfast, proceed for full day excursion to Kufri:

  • Kufri - famous for its Himalayan wildlife zoo
  • Horse ride to Mahasu Peak (optional)
  • Visit Indira Tourist Park
  • On return, visit Jakhoo Temple - dedicated to Lord Hanuman

Overnight stay in Shimla.

After breakfast, check out from hotel and drive to Manali (250 km / 7-8 hrs). Enroute visit:

  • Sundernagar Lake
  • Kullu Valley - famous for its temples and apple orchards
  • Vaishno Devi Temple in Kullu

On arrival in Manali, check in to hotel. Overnight stay in Manali.

Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to visit Himachal depends on what you want to experience:

  • March-June: Pleasant weather, ideal for sightseeing (15°C to 30°C)
  • July-August: Monsoon season with lush greenery
  • September-November: Post-monsoon, great for trekking
  • December-February: Winter season for snow lovers

This package is available year-round except peak winter (Jan-Feb) when some routes may be closed.

Packing depends on the season you're traveling:

  • Summer (Mar-Jun): Light woolens for evenings, comfortable daywear
  • Monsoon (Jul-Aug): Waterproof jacket, quick-dry clothes
  • Winter (Sep-Feb): Heavy woolens, thermals, gloves, muffler

Comfortable walking shoes are recommended year-round as there will be considerable walking during sightseeing.

Yes, this package is suitable for senior citizens with some considerations:

  • The itinerary can be customized to reduce walking

  • Hotels with elevators can be arranged
  • We recommend avoiding winter months (Dec-Jan) due to extreme cold
