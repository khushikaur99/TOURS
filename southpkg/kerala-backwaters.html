Kerala Backwaters Highlights

  • Overnight stay in traditional houseboat (Kettuvallam)
  • Alleppey beach sunset & local fishing village visit
  • Canoe ride through narrow palm-fringed canals
  • Visit to coconut coir making unit & local crafts
  • Authentic Kerala meals cooked onboard
  • Optional Ayurvedic massage experience

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

Morning pickup from Cochin airport/hotel. Drive to Alleppey (2 hours) with stop at Cherai Beach enroute. After lunch, board your private houseboat at 12 PM. Cruise through palm-fringed canals, witnessing local village life. Enjoy sunset views from the deck. Overnight on houseboat anchored in quiet backwaters.

Morning canoe ride through narrow canals inaccessible to houseboats. Visit local village to see coir-making and toddy-tapping demonstrations. Return to houseboat for lunch while cruising. Afternoon visit to Alleppey beach and local fish market. Optional Ayurvedic massage in evening. Overnight at lakeside resort.

After breakfast, drive back to Cochin (2 hours). Enroute visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary (seasonal). In Cochin, tour Fort Kochi including Chinese fishing nets, Jewish Synagogue, and Mattancherry Palace. Evening transfer to airport for departure or extend your stay.

Kerala Backwaters FAQs

Kerala backwaters offer unique experiences throughout the year:

  • September-March: Ideal weather (24°C to 32°C), perfect for cruising
  • April-May: Hotter but fewer crowds, great for photography
  • June-August: Monsoon season - lush greenery but occasional heavy rains
  • December-January: Peak season - pleasant weather but higher prices

Houseboats operate year-round except during extreme weather warnings.

We offer three categories of houseboats (all with private bathrooms):

  • Standard: 1 bedroom, basic amenities, shared crew (₹8,000-12,000/night)
  • Deluxe: 1-2 bedrooms, AC at night, attached sun deck (₹15,000-20,000/night)
  • Premium: 2-3 bedrooms, full AC, luxury furnishings, private chef (₹25,000-40,000/night)

All houseboats include meals and guided village visits.

Essentials for comfortable backwaters experience:

  • Clothing: Light cotton wear, swimwear, sunhat
  • Footwear: Sandals/slippers (shoes removed before boarding)
  • Protection: Sunscreen, sunglasses, mosquito repellent
  • Electronics: Camera, power bank (limited charging on standard boats)
  • Optional: Binoculars for birdwatching, light jacket for evenings

Storage space is limited - pack light in soft bags.

Safety considerations for different age groups:

  • Children: Life jackets available, railings on decks (supervision required)
  • Elderly: Choose premium boats with proper beds and AC
  • Mobility Issues: Boarding requires stepping between boat and shore
  • Medical: Nearest hospital 30-60 mins away by boat+road

We can arrange special assistance with prior notice.

Authentic Kerala cuisine prepared fresh onboard:

  • Meals Included: Breakfast, lunch, dinner + evening tea/snacks
  • Specialties: Karimeen (pearl spot fish), duck roast, avial, appam with stew
  • Vegetarian: Extensive options available (specify when booking)
  • Dietary Needs: Can accommodate requests with advance notice

Bottled water provided; alcohol available for purchase separately.

Popular combination packages:

  • Beach Extension: Add Kovalam/Varkala (2-3 extra days)
  • Hill Station: Munnar tea gardens (2 extra days)
  • Wildlife: Periyar Tiger Reserve (1-2 extra days)
  • Cultural: Kochi heritage tour (1 extra day)
  • Ayurveda: Wellness retreat add-on (3+ extra days)

All extensions include private transfers and guided tours.
