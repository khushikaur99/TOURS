Munnar & Thekkady Highlights

  • Scenic drive through Western Ghats
  • Visit tea plantations & Tea Museum in Munnar
  • Wildlife safari in Periyar Tiger Reserve
  • Spice plantation tour in Thekkady
  • Nature walk & boating in Periyar Lake
  • Optional Ayurvedic massage sessions

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

Pickup from Cochin airport. Scenic 4.5-hour drive to Munnar through hills and waterfalls. Enroute stop at Cheeyappara Waterfalls. Evening at leisure or local walk. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Visit Tea Museum, Eravikulam National Park (subject to availability), Mattupetty Dam & Echo Point. Post-lunch, proceed to Thekkady (approx. 3.5 hours). Check into forest resort. Optional cultural show or massage in evening. Overnight at Thekkady.

Morning spice plantation walk or bamboo rafting (optional). Enjoy a boat ride in Periyar Lake if time permits. Post breakfast, drive back to Cochin (5-6 hrs). Drop at airport/hotel by evening.
